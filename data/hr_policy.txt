Casual Leave Policy:
Employees get 12 casual leaves per year. Casual leave can be used for personal work, family needs, or short planned absences.

Sick Leave Policy:
Employees get 10 sick leaves per year. Sick leave can be used when an employee is unwell or needs medical rest.

Notice Period Policy:
The notice period for employees is 60 days. Employees must complete the notice period before leaving the organization unless management approves early release.

Work From Home Policy:
Employees can work from home 2 days per month with manager approval.

Maternity Leave Policy:
Maternity leave is available as per company policy and applicable government regulations.
